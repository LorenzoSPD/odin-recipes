Pizza Recipe

Description:

What to do when your 8-year old nephew comes to visit? Make pizza, of course!

Well, not of course, actually. I didn't think of it until we exhausted Sorry, Monopoly, and gin rummy. But it did turn out to be a brilliant idea as my father had just received a baking stone for Christmas, and my nephew loves pizza.

Ingredient List:

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato's
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Pizza dough
  • Cheeses

Steps:

  1. Proof the yeast.
  2. Make and knead the pizza dough.
  3. Let the dough rise.
  4. Preheat the pizza stone.
  5. Divide the dough into 2 balls.
  6. Flatten the dough ball, and stretch out into a round.
  7. Brush the dough top with olive oil.
  8. Sprinkle the pizza peel with cornmeal, put flattened dough on top.
  9. Spread with tomato sauce and sprinkle with toppings.
  10. Slide pizza into the oven.
  11. Bake.
